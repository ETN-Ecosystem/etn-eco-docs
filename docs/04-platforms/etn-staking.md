## Why stake instead of just holding?
When you keep $ETN in your wallet, it sits idle. It‚Äôs safe, but it‚Äôs not growing.
Staking puts your $ETN to work. By locking your tokens in the secure ETN-ECO vault, you earn *more* $ETN automatically. It is the difference between keeping cash under your mattress vs. putting it in a high-yield savings account.

### ‚öôÔ∏è How it Works (The Mechanics)

When you open the staking interface, you are making a deal: you promise to lock away your tokens for a set time, and in return, the ecosystem pays you interest (APR).

#### 1. Choose Your Commitment Level (Time)
We offer three "Unstaking periods." The longer you lock your tokens, the higher your reward multiplier.

* **182 Days (6 Months):** Standard rewards. (Current APR: ~10.21%)
* **365 Days (1 Year):** High rewards. You get a 1.9x multiplier on your earnings. (Current APR: ~19.39%)
* **730 Days (2 Years):** Maximum rewards. You get a 2x multiplier. (Current APR: ~20.42%)

*Note: APR (Annual Percentage Rate) fluctuates based on how many people are in the pool.*

#### 2. The Rules of Entry

* **Minimum Deposit:** You need at least 3,000 $ETN to start staking.
* **Deposit Fee:** There is a small 3% fee taken from your deposit immediately. This goes back into the ecosystem/pool health. *Example: If you deposit 3,000, you effectively start staking with 2,910.*

### ‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Early Withdrawal Consequences

This is the most critical part to understand. Staking is designed for Diamond Hands‚Äîpeople committed to the long-term success of ETN.

* **The 20% Unstake Fee:** If you need to access your funds, there is a 20% unstake fee.
* **Why?** This protects the token price and loyal holders from "pump and dump" behavior. It ensures that people don't just stake for a day and leave.
* **Strategy:** Only stake funds you are comfortable not touching for the duration of your chosen period. If you withdraw early, this fee will eat into your principal.

### üìù Step-by-Step for First-Timers

1. **Connect:** Click the "Connect Wallet" button at the top right and select your TON wallet.
2. **Select Period:** Tap on 182, 365, or 730 days. The interface will highlight your choice.
3. **Enter Amount:** Type in how much $ETN you want to stake (or click "Max"). Remember, it must be at least 3,000.
4. **Check Gas:** Ensure you have a small amount of TON (approx 0.5 TON) in your wallet to pay for the blockchain transaction fees.
5. **Stake:** Click the big "Stake" button and confirm the transaction in your wallet app.
